Why I decided to enroll in Epicodus

I enrolled in Epicodus to make a career change. My former career, cooking, was fulfulling in many ways; but it was physically exhausting and time intensive. I felt like I needed a better life/work balance.

I also enrolled because, of the coding I’ve done in the past, I've truly enjoyed it! I can get into a flow state with coding and lose track of time. It feels really good. I enrolled in the camp as an expedient way to learn and get a job. I had been trying to learn on my own before but I realized with my 60-hour work weeks I wouldn't get through as quickly as I wanted.
